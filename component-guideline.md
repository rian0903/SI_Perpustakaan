# Component Guidelines

## Navbar

* Floating
* Transparent on hero
* Glass effect after scrolling
* Height 80px
* Smooth background transition
* Active menu indicator

---

## Hero

One clear message.

One illustration.

Maximum two CTA buttons.

Never place unnecessary content above the fold.

---

## Buttons

Primary

Filled Green

Secondary

Outlined

Ghost

Transparent

Hover

Lift 3px

Scale 1.02

---

## Cards

Rounded

Large image

Minimal text

Consistent padding

Soft shadow

Hover

Translate Y

Scale image

Glow border
